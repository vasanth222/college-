Genesis:-

Dr. Nalla.G.Palaniswami - A name that is taken with great reverence by the medical fraternity and common public alike, for his pioneering efforts in providing quality healthcare. Since its inception in 1989, the Kovai Medical Center and Hospital or KMCH as we know it, has been redefining the corporate hospital set up, making healthcare accessible to all, catering to patients from all over the globe. Looking beyond its success and recognizing the need for competent technical education, Dr.N.G.P initiated the establishment of Dr.N.G.P. Institute of Technology in 2007, and the N.G.P. Group of educational institutions. The College has been approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. It aims to develop and implement a comprehensive Human Resources plan, to include recruitment, professional development, retention and succession plans that attract qualified staff and faculty and ensure that they remain contemporary in their respective roles.

College life

  • College life can be an exciting and transformative time in a person's life. It provides opportunities to learn new things, meet new people, and gain independence and responsibility.
  • Academically, college provides students with the chance to specialize in a subject area of their choosing, take advanced courses, and receive a higher level of education than they would in high school.
  • Socially, college life offers a chance to meet new people from diverse backgrounds, form new relationships, and participate in a variety of clubs and activities. This can be a great time to explore new interests and hobbies and to become involved in the community.
  • In terms of practical skills, college provides students with opportunities to develop time-management, organizational, and critical-thinking skills, as well as to gain real-world experience through internships, co-op programs, or volunteer work.
  • Overall, college life can be challenging, but it is also a time filled with personal and academic growth, and the experiences and relationships formed during this time can last a lifetime.
